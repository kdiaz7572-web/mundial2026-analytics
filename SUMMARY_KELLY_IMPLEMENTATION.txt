================================================================================
FORMULAS IMPLEMENTADAS
================================================================================

Kelly Criterion:
  edge = (probability × odds) - 1
  kelly_% = (edge × probability) / odds

Risk of Ruin (aproximado):
  RoR = (1 - probability) × 100

Bet Size en Colones:
  amount_colones = min((kelly_% / 100) × bankroll, 50000)

FORMULA KELLY - VALIDACION MANUAL
================================================================================

Ejemplo: Argentina 68%, odds 1.80, bankroll ₡40,000

Step 1: Edge
  edge = (0.68 × 1.80) - 1 = 1.224 - 1 = 0.224 = 22.4%

Step 2: Kelly %
  kelly_% = (0.224 × 0.68) / 1.80 = 0.1523 / 1.80 = 0.0846 = 8.46%

Step 3: Bet Size
  amount = 8.46% × ₡40,000 = ₡3,384
